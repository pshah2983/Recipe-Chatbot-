Welcome to the Indian Cuisine Recipe Chatbot!

This chatbot helps you discover and explore Indian recipes using natural language queries. You can search by recipe name, ingredients, cuisine, diet, and more!

Features

  • Intelligent recipe search (by name, ingredient, cuisine, diet, etc.)
  • Image matching for recipes
  • Personalized responses
  • Chat history for logged-in users
  • Modern, responsive interface

How to Use

  1. Type your query in the chat box (e.g., "Show me vegetarian breakfast recipes")
  2. Click send or press Enter
  3. View recipe details and images
